KAIROS

documenting a student-produced musical

book design | printing

dimensions DTL Fleischmann on newsprint, perfect bound. 5x8x0.5"

collaborators Arete Xu

Kairos: the Musical retells the Trojan War myth with themes of non-linear time and entropy with experimental concepts. Documentation of the performance, copies of the actors’ scripts, and personal notes and sketches for planning the musical have been preserved and adapted into limited edition books. All text and images featured are original material; script written by Arete Xu and Waverly Huang.

Book design guided by Anastasiia Raina.
